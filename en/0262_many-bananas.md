# Many Bananas

##
I am hungry!

I want a banana!

##
I go to the tree.

I pluck bananas.

##
I have bananas, now.

##
I eat a banana.

I eat two bananas.

I eat three bananas.

I eat many bananas.

##
I am not hungry, now.

I have a hundred bananas

##
What will I do?

##
I will mix banana and sugar.

I will make banana halwa!

##
I will eat banana halwa.

I am happy!
