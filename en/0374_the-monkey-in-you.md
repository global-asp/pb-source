# The Monkey in You

##
Monkey see,

Monkey do

##
I'm a monkey,

Just like you!

##
Swinging high,

Swinging low,

##
I'm always moving,

On the go

##
Hungry, hungry

All the time

##
Food is always on my mind

##
Making mischief on a tree

##
Takes its toll,

As you can see!
