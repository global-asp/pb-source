# Can and Can't

##
I can't knit, cats can't write, fish can't blink...

##
... pigs can't fly!

##
Dogs can't dance, ants can't read, crows can't sing...

##
... monkeys can't cook!

##
But, monkeys can swing!

##
Crows can caw... ants can bite...

dogs can bark!

##
Pigs can eat, fish can swim, cats can jump...

and I can read!
